Description :   

SHOVL application is a C2C Snow clearance service. Our potential clients such as working professionals, senior individuals, or anyone are busy and unable to clear/shovel the snow after a large snowfall. Users, who are unable to clear the snow for any reason, no longer need to worry about it by using our smartphone application. They can have the service completed at a low cost and with the press of a few buttons.
